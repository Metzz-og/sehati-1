Gym Workout (Strength Training)

Goal: Build muscle and strength
                    Frequency: 3-4 days/week
                    
                    Workout Plan (Full Body)
                    Exercise	Sets x Reps	Notes
                    Squats	3x8-10	Use barbell or dumbbells
                    Bench Press	3x8-10	Barbell or dumbbells
                    Deadlifts	3x5	Focus on form
                    Pull-Ups	3x6-8	Use bands if needed
                    Shoulder Press	3x8-10	Dumbbells or barbell
                    Plank	3x30-60 sec	Core stability
                    Progression:
                    
                    Increase weight by 2.5-5kg every 2 weeks.
                    
                    Reduce rest time (60s → 45s between sets).

Cardio Workout (Fat Loss & Endurance)

Goal: Improve stamina & burn fat
                    Frequency: 3-5 days/week
                    
                    Workout Plan (30-Minute Session)
                    Warm-Up (5 min) – Brisk walk or light jog
                    
                    Main Workout (20 min) – Choose one:
                    
                    Running (Outdoor/Treadmill) – Moderate pace
                    
                    Cycling (Stationary/Outdoor) – Keep RPM > 70
                    
                    Jump Rope – 30 sec on, 30 sec off
                    
                    Cool Down (5 min) – Slow walk + stretching
                    
                    Progression:
                    
                    Increase duration by 5 min weekly.
                    
                    Add intervals (e.g., 1 min sprint, 2 min jog).

Stretching Routine (Flexibility & Mobility)

Goal: Improve flexibility & reduce stiffness
                    Frequency: Daily (5-10 min)
                    
                    Stretching Plan (Hold Each for 20-30 sec)
                    Stretch	Target Area
                    Hamstring Stretch	Legs (back thigh)
                    Quad Stretch	Front thighs
                    Chest Opener	Shoulders & chest
                    Cat-Cow	Spine mobility
                    Butterfly Stretch	Hips & groin
                    Neck Rolls	Neck tension
                    Progression:
                    
                    Hold stretches for longer (30s → 45s).
                    
                    Add dynamic stretches (leg swings, arm circles).

Athleticism Workout (Speed & Agility)

Goal: Boost explosiveness & coordination
                    Frequency: 2-3 days/week
                    
                    Workout Plan (20-30 min)
                    Warm-Up (5 min) – Jumping jacks, high knees
                    
                    Drills:
                    
                    Sprints – 5x20m (full recovery)
                    
                    Lateral Shuffles – 3x30 sec
                    
                    Box Jumps – 3x8
                    
                    Burpees – 3x10
                    
                    Cool Down (5 min) – Light jog + stretching
                    
                    Progression:
                    
                    Increase sprint distance (20m → 40m).
                    
                    Reduce rest between drills (60s → 30s).
